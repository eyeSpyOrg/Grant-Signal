EYE SPY GRANT SCOUT - QUICK START
==================================

FIRST TIME ONLY:
  1. If this arrived as a ZIP file: right-click it, choose
     "Extract All...", and use the extracted folder.
  2. Install Python (free): go to  python.org/downloads  and click
     the yellow "Download Python" button. When the installer opens,
     TICK THE CHECKBOX "Add python.exe to PATH" on the first screen
     (bottom of the window), then click "Install Now".

EVERY TIME:
  3. Double-click the file called  run.bat
     A black window opens, then your web browser opens the app.

  - Keep the black window open while you use the app.
  - To stop, close the black window.
  - Your saved work stays on this computer between sessions.

FIRST THING TO DO IN THE APP:
  On the Dashboard, click "Index all starter funders now".
  This loads grant histories for 11 Jacksonville-area and
  vision-focused foundations (takes a few minutes, one time).

Problems? Open README.md - it has a troubleshooting table.
