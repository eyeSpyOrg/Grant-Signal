How it works

  • Private foundations (Form 990-PF) must list every grant they pay — recipient, location, purpose, amount.
  • Public charities (Form 990, Schedule I) list grants of $5,000+ to organizations.
  • The indexer pulls each funder’s {{ 6 }} most recent e-filed returns and stores the grants locally.
  • Everything is cached — re-indexing a funder only fetches new filings.
